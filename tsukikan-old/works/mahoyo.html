Mahou Tsukai no Yoru

Witch on the Holy Night

One of Nasu's earliest works, featuring Aozaki Aoko as the main character, rumor has it only a few TYPE-MOON members have read it. Some of the characters from the story were drawn by Takeuchi for his old personal website.

In April 2008, a game version of Mahou Ysukai no Yoru was announced as part of "the next worlds of TYPE-MOON." Unlike TYPE-MOON's previous games, Mahou Tsukiai no Yoru will not be an ero-game, and will feature Koyama Hirokazu as the lead artist in place of Takeuchi. The game was set to release sometime in 2009. After several delays, the game received a September 2010 release date. In September, however, TYPE-MOON announced that the release date has been pushed back to 2010 winter, and later TBA 2011.
