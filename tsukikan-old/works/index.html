Works

Tsukihime
An 18+ visual novel made during TYPE-MOON's doujin days. One of the most successful doujin game of its time, and was adapted into an anime, manga, and more. The work that helped secured TYPE-MOON's position as one of the most popular groups (and later company) in the scene.
Fate/stay night
TYPE-MOON's first game as a commercial company. An 18+ visual novel that was extremely popular and spawned many spin-off series and merchandise, including an anime adaptation and a novel prequel.
Mahou Tsukai no Yoru
TYPE-MOON's newest game. Based on one of Nasu's early novel of the same name, it's the first main TYPE-MOON game to not feature any 18+ content. Currently set to release in 2011.
Kara no Kyoukai
One of Nasu's most popular novels. Published by Kodansha after first being released by TYPE-MOON as a doujin novel. It was later adapted into a 7-part animated movie, which helped the series gain mainstream attention and success.
DDD
A mystery novel written by Nasu published by Kodansha.
Melty Blood
A very popular 2D fighting game that was the result of a collaboration between TYPE-MOON and French Bread. It was later ported to the arcade and PS2.
Girls' Work
A new game announced in late 2008. Not much has been revealed since then other than an anime adaptation.
